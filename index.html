START - BETTER BULB

Tulip bulbs are measured in centimeters around the "waist" of the bulb. Longfield Gardens supplies tulip bulbs that are at least 12cm in circumference. This ensures you will always get the biggest, brightest blooms.

PLAN FOR SUCCESS

Tulips are remarkably versatile and will grow in sun, part sun or shade.Tulips should be planted in good soil that is well-drained and never soggy. Peat moss or compost may be added to help improve drainage.

CUT FLOWER GARDENS

Enjoy the fun of arranging bouquets of tulips for your home or to share with friends. Planting tulip bulbs in a cutting garden makes it easy to bring the freshness of spring indoors.
